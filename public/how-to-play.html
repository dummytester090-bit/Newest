How to Play (Get Started)

Step‑by‑Step Guide

  1. Choose a key type – Basic (10 min), Standard (30 min), or Good (1 hour). The longer the key, the more time you have to use the AI tools.
  2. Wait for the timer – Each key has a mandatory wait time (5, 10, or 20 minutes). This ensures fair distribution of resources. You can speed it up with the ⚡ button.
  3. Click “Get Key” – Once the timer reaches zero, the button becomes active. Click it to generate your unique key. Copy it immediately.
  4. Join our Discord – Use the link in the footer or on the homepage to join the Halurea Discord server.
  5. Use your key – In the Discord server, go to the #key‑access channel. Type /redeem YOUR-KEY-HERE and press Enter. You’ll be granted access to the AI channels.
  6. Start creating! – Head to the unlocked channels (e.g., #text‑to‑image, #image‑to‑video) and use the commands. For example, /imagine prompt: a beautiful sunset.

Important Notes

  • Keys expire after the validity period. If you need more time, generate a new key.
  • You can use the ⚡ button multiple times – each click reduces the timer by 2 seconds (with a 1‑second cooldown).
  • Your key is single‑use. After redeeming it, you cannot reuse it.
  • If you encounter issues, check the Tips & Tricks or ask in the Discord support channel.
