How to Get Started

  1. Choose a key type – Basic (10 min), Standard (30 min), or Good (1 hour).
  2. Wait for the timer – The countdown ensures fair distribution. You can speed it up with the ⚡ button (see Tips & Tricks).
  3. Get your key – Once the timer hits zero, click “Get Key”. A unique key will appear. Copy it.
  4. Join our Discord – Use the key in the #key‑access channel to unlock AI tool channels.
  5. Start creating – Use commands in the unlocked channels to generate images, videos, and more.

That’s it! No account, no email – just pure AI fun.

Pro Tips for Faster Access
