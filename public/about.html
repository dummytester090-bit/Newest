About Halurea

Halurea is a project developed to provide easy access to AI tools through a key system. The main focus of this project is to build a platform that is lightweight, fast, and easy to use for everyone.

We believe that AI technology should be accessible to everyone without having to go through complex processes such as registration or payment.

With this approach, Halurea provides a more open and flexible user experience to users.
