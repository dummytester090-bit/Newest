Tips & Tricks

Speed Up Your Waiting Time

The ⚡ button is your best friend. Each click reduces the remaining timer by 2 seconds. Because it has a 1‑second cooldown, you can click it many times in quick succession to drastically cut down the wait. For a 5‑minute timer (300 seconds), you could theoretically finish in less than 3 minutes by clicking 150 times. Try it!

Mastering Text‑to‑Image Prompts

Use detailed descriptions: “a majestic dragon in a medieval castle, hyper‑detailed, oil painting, dramatic lighting”. Add artistic styles: “in the style of Van Gogh”, “cyberpunk”, “photorealistic”. Use negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements: “no blur, no watermark”.

Image‑to‑Video Enhancements

For smoother motion, describe the movement explicitly: “water flowing gently”, “leaves blowing in the wind”, “character smiling slowly”. You can also chain multiple videos by feeding the output back into the tool.

Motion Control Shortcuts

Use coordinates to define paths: “from (0,0) to (100,100) over 5 seconds”. Combine with emotions: “angry movement”, “happy dance”. Experiment with different interpolation modes (linear, bezier).

Community Engagement

Join the Discord early, even while waiting. You can watch others’ creations, get inspired, and ask questions. Many experienced users share their prompts and settings – a great way to learn.

Managing Multiple Keys

If you need extended sessions, generate a new key before your current one expires. You can stack keys by redeeming them one after another.
