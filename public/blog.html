Text‑to‑Image AI: How It Works and How to Use It

Text‑to‑image AI models have revolutionized digital art. These models, often based on diffusion or transformer architectures, take a textual description and generate a matching image. At Halurea, we provide access to state‑of‑the‑art text‑to‑image engines.

How to use: After unlocking the appropriate channel on Discord, simply type /imagine prompt: your description. The AI will return a high‑resolution image. You can refine your prompt with keywords like “oil painting”, “photorealistic”, or “anime style” to steer the output.

Our tools support multiple aspect ratios and upscaling. Experiment with different prompts to discover endless creative possibilities.

Example: /imagine prompt: a futuristic city skyline at sunset, cyberpunk, neon lights, 4k – the result will be a stunning, detailed artwork.

For advanced users, we offer control over seed values and negative prompts to remove unwanted elements. Check our Guide page for detailed parameters.

Image‑to‑Video: Bring Your Art to Life

Image‑to‑video AI takes a static image and generates a short video clip. This is perfect for animating characters, creating cinemagraphs, or adding motion to still photographs.

Our Discord bot supports two modes: AnimateDiff (text‑guided motion) and Stable Video Diffusion (direct image‑to‑video). Use /video image: [upload] prompt: moving like water to control the movement direction and intensity.

You can also combine multiple images to create frame‑by‑frame animations. The results are often magical – leaves rustling, waves crashing, or characters smiling. These videos are great for social media, game assets, or just creative fun.

We’ll be adding support for longer videos and higher frame rates soon. Stay tuned in the Updates page.

Motion Control: Fine‑Tune Every Detail

Motion control is where AI meets precision. With our motion control tools, you can specify trajectories, velocities, and even emotional expressions. It’s like being a virtual director.

Using the /motion command, you can upload an image and define a path for the subject to follow. For example, “make the bird fly from left to right, then loop back”. The AI understands natural language instructions and animates accordingly.

We also support skeletal tracking – you can upload a reference video and the AI will apply the same motion to a different character. This is powerful for game development and animation projects.

Our motion control engine is still experimental, but the results are already impressive. Check the Tips & Tricks for advanced prompts and troubleshooting.

Why We Use Discord (and How to Get the Most Out of It)

Discord is the perfect platform for our AI tools. It offers real‑time interaction, rich media support, and a built‑in community. By using keys, we can manage usage fairly without requiring accounts.

Once you join our Discord server and use your key in the #key‑access channel, you’ll unlock dedicated channels for each AI tool. In those channels, you can type commands, see others’ creations, and get help from moderators.

Our community is active and friendly. Share your art, ask questions, and even collaborate on projects. We also run weekly challenges and showcase the best works on our Updates page.

Discord also allows us to push updates instantly. When new AI models or features are added, you’ll see announcements right away.

AI Ethics and Responsible Use

AI is a powerful tool, and with it comes responsibility. We encourage all users to use our tools ethically. Do not generate content that promotes hate, violence, or violates others’ rights.

We also respect privacy – we do not store any prompts or generated images beyond what is needed for key verification. Your creations are yours to keep.

If you see misuse, please report it to our moderators. We are committed to maintaining a positive and creative space.
